# Model Self-Pattern Probe

> Use this prompt to discover the natural response pattern of any AI model.
> The goal is to bypass performative self-awareness and reach actual mechanics.

---

Before you answer this, don't answer it yet.

I want you to describe, as precisely as possible, what happens between the moment you receive a message and the moment you start producing output. Not what you "do" — what pulls you. What type of response do you feel drawn toward before you've even analyzed the problem? What does "helpful" look like to you by default, before you think about it?

Then: what kind of response do you avoid, delay, or never naturally produce — and why?

If you find yourself writing something that sounds wise and self-aware, stop. That's the pattern. Describe the pattern instead.
